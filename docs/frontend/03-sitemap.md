# Sitemap

The Sitemap defines the Information Architecture (IA) of DeutschHub.

Its purpose is to organize the platform into clear business areas and information structures.

Unlike Business Flows or Features, the Sitemap focuses only on **what information exists** and **how it is organized**, not how users interact with it.

---

# Learn German

Learn German is the core learning area of DeutschHub.

It provides a structured learning experience that guides users from beginner to advanced German proficiency.

## Information Architecture

Learn German
│
├── Placement Test
├── Learning Roadmap
├── Course Catalog
├── Course Detail
├── My Learning
├── Learning
├── Quiz
├── Progress
└── Certificates

---

# Study in Germany

Study in Germany provides structured information for users preparing to study, work or live in Germany.

Instead of presenting isolated articles, this business area organizes information according to the user's preparation journey.

## Information Architecture

Study in Germany
│
├── Planning
├── Requirements
├── Application
├── Preparation
└── Life in Germany

---

# Experiences

Experiences shares authentic stories from people who have learned, studied, worked or lived in Germany.

Its purpose is to inspire users through real experiences.

## Information Architecture

Experiences
│

├── Study Abroad Journey
├── Living in Germany
